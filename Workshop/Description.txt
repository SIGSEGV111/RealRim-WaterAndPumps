[h1][RealRim] Water & Pumps[/h1]

Physics-oriented replacement for the water, hot-water, heating, sewage and air-conditioning systems in Dubs Bad Hygiene.

[h2]Independent networks[/h2]
[list]
[*]Fresh/cold water
[*]Domestic hot water
[*]Heating water
[*]Waste water
[*]Air-con coolant
[/list]

[h2]Metric physical accounting[/h2]
[list]
[*]Pump flow in L/h and electrical input in W/kW
[*]Water and wastewater storage in liters
[*]Sludge in kilograms and 50 g units
[*]Heat/cooling transfer in kW
[*]Tank, room and pool temperatures in °C
[*]Fuel energy based on 50 g chemfuel and 400 g wood units
[/list]

Includes realistic pump values, long-network flow loss, thermal tanks, boilers, solar/geothermal heating, two heat-pump types, ΔT radiators, phase-change coolant storage, demand-driven air conditioning, detailed pool thermodynamics, body-size-based thirst, trough storage, refillable independent latrines, active-stove kitchen-sink accounting, trait-aware water preferences, automatic pawn sludge extraction, septic infiltration and sewage recovery.

Water sources now carry hidden, persistent pathogen profiles. Contamination mixes by volume through tanks and troughs, drinking applies dose-based disease rolls to pawns and animals, and a powered water-treatment unit removes 99% of pathogens from delivered water.

The mod reuses DBH buildings where practical and redirects verified DBH jobs/callbacks to the new resource networks. This is a clean 1.1 rewrite and is not intended to load 1.0.x saves unchanged.
